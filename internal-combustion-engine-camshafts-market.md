<p><strong>The Internal Combustion Engine Camshafts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Overhead Camshaft (SOHC)</li><li>Double Overhead Camshaft (DOHC)</li><li>Overhead Valve (OHV) Camshafts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The internal combustion engine camshaft market is primarily categorized into three types: Single Overhead Camshaft (SOHC), Double Overhead Camshaft (DOHC), and Overhead Valve (OHV) camshafts. SOHC engines feature one camshaft for both intake and exhaust valves, providing simplicity and cost-effectiveness. DOHC engines have separate camshafts for each function, allowing for improved performance and efficiency. OHV camshafts, located in the engine block, operate pushrods to control valves, offering advantages in torque and compact design, appealing for various automotive applications.</p></p>
