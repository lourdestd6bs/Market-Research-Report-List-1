<p><p>Aramid fibers are high-performance synthetic fibers known for their strength, heat resistance, and durability. The market for aramid fibers is primarily divided into two types: para-aramid fibers and meta-aramid fibers. Para-aramid fibers, such as Kevlar, are renowned for their exceptional tensile strength and are widely used in ballistic protection and aerospace applications. Meta-aramid fibers, on the other hand, offer excellent thermal stability and are commonly utilized in applications like electrical insulation, firefighting gear, and industrial protective clothing.</p></p>
