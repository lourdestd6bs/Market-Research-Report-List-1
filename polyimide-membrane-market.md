<p><strong>The Polyimide Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Both Benzenoid</li><li>Biphenyl Polyimide Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyimide membrane market includes two primary types: benzenoid and biphenyl polyimide membranes. Benzenoid polyimides are derived from aromatic compounds and exhibit excellent thermal stability, mechanical strength, and chemical resistance, making them suitable for various industrial applications. On the other hand, biphenyl polyimides incorporate biphenyl structures, offering enhanced gas permeability and selectivity, which are beneficial for applications like filtration and separation processes. Both types are widely utilized across sectors such as electronics, aerospace, and automotive.</p></p>
