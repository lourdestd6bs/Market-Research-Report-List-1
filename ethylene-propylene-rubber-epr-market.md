<p><p>Ethylene Propylene Rubber (EPR) encompasses various types, primarily Ethylene Propylene Monomer (EPM) and Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer (EPDM). EPM is a saturated rubber characterized by excellent weather resistance and flexibility, making it suitable for electrical insulation and automotive applications. EPDM, on the other hand, includes diene for enhanced cross-linking, providing superior thermal stability, UV resistance, and aging properties. Both types are widely used in automotive, construction, and industrial sectors, catering to diverse performance requirements.</p></p>
